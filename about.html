Hey, I'm Ivan — most people online know me as IVXNCHIS. I'm based in Bucaramanga, Colombia, and by day I lead a customer service team in the online gaming industry. Here's a bit more of the story than the resume tells.

How I got here

I got into this industry through a referral from a really good friend, so I already had some sense of what the work looked like before I started. But it wasn't until I was actually in it that I fell in love with the job — specifically the psychology behind it. It's fascinating how small changes in how you talk to someone, how you time a message, or how you read what a player actually needs can be the difference between a small deposit and a big one. That mix of people-reading and strategy is what's kept me hooked, and it's why I've stuck around long enough to go from agent to leading a team of my own.
